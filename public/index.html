A New Calendar For Alzheimer's Comming Soon

About Us

Akal is a new system which aims to help caretakers schedule around an Alzheimer's patient's life.

Our process

We have conducted a study to ensure akal is the perfect solution for caretakers. With the full release, we will also release the API to build your own ideas on top of the system.

Our Vision

Akal aims to create a helpful and affordable solution for caretakers of Alzheimer's and dementia patients by means of our electronic calendar system.

Our Approach

We approached this solution with the main goal of making it affordable to everyone. We ensure to use high quality parts, but getting them at a fair price.

Our Objective

Our objective is to gather data for caretakers about their patients environments and help to not only schedule their lives, but also to help with the patients repetitive question disorders.
